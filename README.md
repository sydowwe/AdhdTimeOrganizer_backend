# ADHD Time Organizer

ADHD Time Organizer is a comprehensive application designed to help individuals with ADHD manage their time effectively. The app includes features such as a to-do list, routine to-do list, task planner, activity history, and more.

## Features

- **To-Do List**: Create and manage your daily tasks.
- **Routine To-Do List**: Set up and track routine tasks that need to be done regularly.
- **Task Planner**: Plan and organize your tasks for better time management.
- **Activity History**: Keep a record of your completed activities and tasks.
- **User Management**: Securely manage user accounts and authentication.
- **Google reCAPTCHA Integration**: Protect your application from spam and abuse.

## Privacy Notice

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. For both Google account registration and normal registration, we only store your email address. No other personal data is collected or stored.

To keep your data secure, we highly recommend activating two-factor authentication (2FA).
